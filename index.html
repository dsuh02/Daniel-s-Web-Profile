    CovidHacks

    Covid-19 is a calamity that is affecting everyone, indiscriminate to age, nationality, or anything that divides us -- that's why CovidHacks was created. CovidHacks is a hackathon that aims to draw youth programming enthusiasts from all around the globe together to develop innovative solutions to the challenges caused by the COVID-19 experience. This event took place for 24 hours, and it was open to students of all skill levels.

    Over these 24 hours, 3 different workshops were hosted for any participants who wished to attend:
    “Build a Cloud-Connected AR/VR App in 15 Minutes or Less” by EchoAR
    "Intro to Firebase Client” by STAC Programming
    “How to be Financially Literate” by FLEO

    Images above: Opening Ceremony, Sponsors List, Prizes List, Closing Ceremony, DEVPOST Page.
